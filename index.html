Ecosistema de Emprendimiento Social

El caso de Bogota

Acerca del proyecto

Desarrollado en 2018, este proyecto pretende dar una mirada al Ecosistema de Emprendimiento social. The case study is built after a deep analysis of 17 semi-structured interviews and 21 documents; with an additional review of more than 100 websites. The analysed data presents a social entrepreneurial ecosystem with diverse actors and some efforts to create a public policy around it, with the potential of being sustainable
